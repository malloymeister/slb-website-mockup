The SLB Difference

What We Do

Sale Leasebacks Across Every Asset Class

Sale leasebacks are M&A-light transactions that require deep expertise in both corporate finance and real estate. We advise businesses on unlocking the capital tied up in their owned real estate — across restaurants, manufacturing, healthcare, industrial, and more — while retaining full operational control through a long-term lease.
